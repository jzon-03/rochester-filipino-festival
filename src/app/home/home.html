Welcome to Rochester Filipino Festival

The Rochester Filipino Festival is a vibrant celebration of Filipino culture, heritage, and community in Upstate New York. Join us for a weekend filled with traditional performances, authentic cuisine, arts and crafts, and family-friendly activities. Experience the warmth and hospitality of the Filipino community while enjoying the rich cultural traditions that make our heritage unique.

Filipino American Association of Rochester (FAAR)

The Filipino American Association of Rochester (FAAR) is dedicated to promoting Filipino culture, heritage, and community engagement in the Greater Rochester area. Through events like the Rochester Filipino Festival, FAAR brings together Filipino Americans and the broader community to celebrate our rich traditions, cuisine, and vibrant culture.

Learn more about FAAR and get involved: info@filamroc.org | www.filamroc.org
